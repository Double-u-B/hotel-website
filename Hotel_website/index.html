      Welcome

      Discover a unique place by the sea.

      The newly opened Jumeirah Hotel will welcome you with a fantastic location 20 m from the sandy beach, a unique panorama of the ocean and a peaceful, relaxing environment of spa greenery.

      Add to this a wealth of attractions, delicious cuisine, an exclusive Wellness & Spa zone, the highest standard of service - as well as a few surprises - and see for yourself that it is the perfect place for a family vacation, a romantic getaway for two, an active fit-style weekend, a business meeting, training or conference.

      -ROOMS-

      Comfort for the whole family

      The Jumeirah hotel offers more then 50 comfortable rooms and suites occupying an area between 27 and 45 square meters. All rooms have a balcony, air conditioning and kitchenette, 55” TV and free wireless Internet.

      -RESTAURANT-

      On-The-Wave

      Take a culinary journey through the flavors of the world.

      Sea air stimulates the appetite. In the On-The-Wave restaurant you will flow on a wave of culinary inspirations - both those known, traditional and surprising exotic fusion of flavors and aromas.

      Our menu changes depending on the season, but you will always find dishes based on fresh, natural products from regional suppliers. There are also vegetarian and vegan offers, and especially for allergy sufferers.

      -WELLNESS & SPA-

      For health and beauty

      Open yourself to the invigorating power of nature. In search of inner balance and relief, you can gaze at the sea endlessly - or visit the exclusive Wellness & Spa zone

      There you will find a swimming pool, sauna area, Jacuzzi, cryochamber, tepidarium, as well as a carefully selected collection of facial and body treatments as well as massages and rituals inspired by nature - including sauna ceremonies or rituals of brewing and drinking tea. All this so that the body, mind and spirit are combined into one, harmonious and relaxed whole.
